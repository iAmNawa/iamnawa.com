About Planaby...
At Planaby, we believe there’s just too many great gems and events and ideas of things to do, we simply have no way to keep track of what’s going on around us. It’s become kind of a drag. Here we are, in New York City, and there’s so much to do —we just can’t seem to do anything more than the same old thing. Break your routine! Meet other fun people! Get outside your apartment and make some plans! At Planaby, we believe the world should be more social and that people need to waste less time organizing their day and more enjoying it. Tell us what you’d like to see! Did we miss your favorite museum? Are there outdoor movies we’ve not listed? Wine tastings in your neighborhood people should know about? Just drop us a line and we’ll get right on it!.
